About Me

Franklin Wilborn Founded ConsumeSmarter in 2009 with a Vision of Empowering Consumers with Online Spend Management Tools. He currently oversees the day to day operations and development of ConsumeSmarter. Franklin holds a B.S. in Economics with a concentration in Finance from Emory University. His Interest include, Computer Programming, Spending Time with Family & Friends, and Showing his Beloved World-Class Rottweilers. Franklin is from Olympia Fields, Illinois.
